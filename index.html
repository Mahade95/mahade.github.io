About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Computer Application Technology at the School of Software, Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology (NUIST), China. My research focuses on evolutionary neural architecture search, medical image analysis, deep learning, computer vision, explainable artificial intelligence, predictive analytics, and healthcare-oriented intelligent systems.

I actively contribute to the scientific community through peer-review activities, conference service, and interdisciplinary research in artificial intelligence, computer vision, and medical image computing.
