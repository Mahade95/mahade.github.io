About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Computer Application Technology at the School of Software, Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology (NUIST), China. My research focuses on evolutionary neural architecture search, medical image analysis, deep learning, computer vision, explainable artificial intelligence, and predictive analytics. I am actively involved in academic research, peer review activities, and the development of intelligent healthcare systems through machine learning and data-driven approaches.
